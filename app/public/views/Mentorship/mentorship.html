A Great Way to
Get the Mentorship
You Need

We believe that there is something powerful and empowering in individuals with disabilities supporting other individuals with disabilities. Our mentorship program serves post secondary education students with disabilities. Students have access to mentors who are university or college graduates with disabilities to support them to meet their goals.

Online
Mentorship

Complete our mentorship program online with an easy-to-use timeline to help guide you along the way!

A dashboard to keep you organized

As a mentor or mentee, you’ll have access to your own dashboard loaded with all the tools and resources you‘ll need for a successful mentorship experience.

Your own personal timeline

Keep track of your mentorship journey with an easy-to-use timeline to track your progress, activities and meetings with your mentor or mentee.

Step-by-step stages to guide you

Your timeline includes the stages of the program so you’ll know exactly what activities are recommended at each stage of the mentorship cycle.

Schedule
Appointments

With our integrated calendar, it’s easy to schedule appointments with your mentor.

Convenient scheduler

Using a calendar application, mentor–mentee matches can schedule real time meetings.

Let your mentee know when you’re available

Mentors can use the calendar application to let their mentee know when exactly they are available to meet.

Meet when it’s convenient

The calendar application lets mentees know exactly when their mentor is available so they can choose a meeting time that is convenient for them!

Chat with
Your Mentor

Gain direct access to your mentor through your own private chat room.

A private chatroom

Each mentor-mentee match has their own private chatroom they can use to text each other in real time.

Security features to protect your privacy

Your chatroom can only be access by you and your match. To make your information even more secure, we’ve password protected your chats.

Forgot to logout? We’ve got you covered

As an additional security feature, chatroom sessions are set to automatically logout after a period of inactivity if you forget to.
