Learning Modules

Our series of learning modules offer a convenient way to learn about topics related to disability and post-secondary education.

Each module is designed for ease of use and is adaptable. Learners can increase or decrease the text side and change the colour. Learners with an account on CommunAbility.ca can stop and then later return to the place where they left off. Our modules are also screen-reader friendly.

The list of topics covered is growing so check back often.
